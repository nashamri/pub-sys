Guidelines

Submission Guidelines

To ensure a smooth submission process, please follow these guidelines:

  • All submissions must be original, unpublished work
  • Articles must be submitted in PDF format
  • Include a clear title, abstract, and keywords
  • Provide complete author information and affiliations
  • Ensure all references are properly cited

Formatting Requirements

Please adhere to the following formatting guidelines:

  • Use standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12pt)
  • Double-space the entire document
  • Include page numbers
  • Use standard margins (1 inch/2.54 cm)

Peer Review Process

Our review process follows these steps:

  1. Initial screening by the editorial team
  2. Assignment to appropriate reviewers
  3. Double-blind peer review
  4. Editorial decision based on reviewer feedback
  5. Author revisions (if required)
  6. Final acceptance and publication

Review Criteria

Manuscripts are evaluated based on:

  • Originality and significance of the research
  • Methodological rigor
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Relevance to the field
  • Quality of analysis and conclusions

Publication Ethics

We adhere to the highest standards of publication ethics:

  • Authors must declare any conflicts of interest
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited
  • Data fabrication and falsification are unacceptable
  • Authorship must be properly attributed
  • Research involving human subjects must follow ethical guidelines

Open Access Policy

Our journal operates under an open access model:

  • Articles are freely available to readers worldwide
  • Authors retain copyright of their work
  • Articles are published under Creative Commons licenses
  • No article processing charges (APCs) for authors
